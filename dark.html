Diffuse Nebulae

Diffuse Nebulae are usually categorized into Emission and Reflection Nebulae. Emission Nebula are clouds of ionized gas that emit their own light at optical wavelengths. A Reflection Nebula reflects light from nearby stars. Lagoon Nebula, Bubble Nebula, Omega Nebula and Orion Nebula are some of the many Diffuse Nebulae.
